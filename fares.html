Metro Sarthi Fares

Standard Fares

The fares for the metro sarthi are determined based on the distance traveled. The minimum fare is ₹10 and the maximum fare is ₹60.

Fare Structure

Distance (km) Fare (₹)
0 - 2 10
2 - 5 20
5 - 12 30
12 - 21 40
21+ 60

Smart Card Details

The metro sarthi Smart Card is a convenient and hassle-free way to travel on the metro. It offers various benefits over single journey tickets, including discounts and ease of use.

Benefits of Using a Smart Card

  • Discounted fares compared to single journey tickets.
  • Quick access through dedicated smart card entry and exit gates.
  • No need to queue for tickets; just recharge your card.
  • Validity of 10 years, making it a long-term travel solution.
  • Ability to use the card for multiple journeys.

How to Obtain a Smart Card

Smart Cards can be purchased at all metro stations. To obtain a card, visit any ticket counter and:

  1. Fill out the application form.
  2. Provide a passport-sized photograph.
  3. Pay a nominal fee for the card, which includes an initial balance.

Recharge Information

You can recharge your Smart Card at:

  • Metro station ticket counters.
  • Automatic Ticket Vending Machines (ATVMs).
  • Online through the metro sarthi website or mobile app.

Usage Instructions

To use the Smart Card:

  1. Tap the card at the entry gate to unlock it.
  2. Travel to your destination station.
  3. Tap the card at the exit gate to complete your journey.
